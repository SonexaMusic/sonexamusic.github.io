# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

Sonexa Music is designed with a local-first and privacy-focused approach. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

## 1. Data Collection

Sonexa Music does **not collect or store personal data by default**.

The app primarily operates using:
- Local audio files stored on your device
- User-defined settings and preferences

We do not require account creation or personal identification to use core features.

## 2. Local Data

All of the following remain on your device:
- Music library and metadata
- Playback history
- Equalizer settings
- App preferences

This data is never transmitted unless explicitly required by a feature.

## 3. Optional Network Features

Some features may require internet access, including:
- Lyrics fetching
- Optional streaming integrations (e.g., YouTube Music, JioSaavn, Tidal)

When these features are used:
- Requests are made directly to third-party services
- Sonexa does not store or control third-party responses

## 4. Third-Party Services

Sonexa may interact with third-party services. These services have their own privacy policies.

We are not responsible for:
- Data collected by third-party APIs
- Content served by external platforms

## 5. No Tracking or Analytics

Sonexa Music does **not use**:
- Tracking systems
- Behavioral analytics
- Advertising identifiers

## 6. Permissions

Sonexa Music may request certain permissions to provide core functionality and enhanced features. These may include:

- **Storage Access** – To scan, read, and play audio files stored on your device  
- **Media Library Access** – To organize and display your music collection  
- **Network Access** – For optional features such as lyrics, streaming integrations, and updates  
- **Foreground Service / Background Playback** – To allow continuous music playback when the app is minimized or the device is locked  
- **Notification Access** – To display playback controls and media information in system notifications  
- **Battery Optimization Exemption (Optional)** – To ensure uninterrupted playback and prevent the system from stopping the app in the background  
- **Audio Settings / Effects Control** – To apply equalizer settings and audio enhancements  

All permissions are:
- Used strictly for their intended functionality  
- Never used for tracking or data collection  
- Requested only when required by specific features  

Users retain full control and may deny permissions, though some features may not function as intended.

## 7. Data Security

Since most data remains on your device, security depends on your device environment.

We do not store user data on remote servers.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Sonexa does not knowingly collect data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated as features evolve. Continued use of the app implies acceptance of updates.
